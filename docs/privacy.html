ColorPick Lens Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Data Collection

ColorPick Lens does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data (including color palettes and settings) is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

2. Data Storage

The following data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Color palettes
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Extension settings

3. Permissions Usage

The extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • Storage: For saving your color palettes and preferences locally
  • ClipboardWrite: For copying color values to clipboard
  • ActiveTab: For color picking and webpage analysis
  • Scripting: For analyzing webpage colors

4. Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, contact us at: www.digitalcloud.no
